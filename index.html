School Uniform

Summer season

This is the uniform of summer season of our school. In summer season junior class girls wear legins, shirt and tulip with tie, belt and id card and the senior class girls wear suit, patiala and duppatta with tie and id card. Boys wear pant and shirt with tie, belt and id card.

Winter season

This is the uniform of winter season of our school. In winter season junior class girls wear legins, shirt, tulip, half sweater and coat with tie, belt and id card and the senior class girls wear suit, patiala, duppatta and coat with id card. Boys wear pant, shirt, half sweater and coat with tie, belt and id card.
